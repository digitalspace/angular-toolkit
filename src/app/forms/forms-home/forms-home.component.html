NGDS Forms are a collection of UI input components that enhance the existing Angular Forms functionality. At a minimum, you must provide every NGDS input with an Angular AbstractControl or derivative. All other attributes are optional. You can manage the value, state, and other form logic from the AbstractControl in TypeScript realm, and the NGDS Forms will adapt in HTML.
